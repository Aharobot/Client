libSgvX3D - An X3D manipulator in SIGVerse
==========================================

How to build libSgvX3D
======================
libSgvX3D organizes three-dimensional data for SIGVerse by
manipulating X3D parser(more on that later) written by Java.
To build libSgvX3D, JDK(JavaSE) 1.6 or later is required.
